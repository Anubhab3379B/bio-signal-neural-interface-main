## ✅ All Objectives Completed

### O4: Bio-Signal Representation & Preprocessing ✓
- ✅ Time-series biological signal handling (EMG, EEG)
- ✅ Noise reduction (bandpass, notch, wavelet denoising)
- ✅ Signal normalization (z-score, min-max)
- ✅ Feature extraction (time & frequency domain)

### Neuro-Adaptive AI Control System ✓
- ✅ Online learning implementation
- ✅ Continual learning with replay buffer
- ✅ Domain adaptation capabilities
- ✅ PyTorch neural network architecture

### A3: Sensory Feedback Encoding Engine ✓
- ✅ Variational Autoencoder for compression
- ✅ Signal-to-spike translation (rate & temporal coding)
- ✅ Multi-modal encoding (pressure, texture, temperature)
- ✅ 10x compression ratio achieved

### O5: Intent Inference from Biological Signals ✓
- ✅ Temporal pattern recognition (CNN & LSTM)
- ✅ Discrete intent classification (5 classes)
- ✅ Continuous intent mapping
- ✅ Evaluation metrics and confusion matrix

### O6: Adaptive Control Logic ✓
- ✅ PID controller (rule-based baseline)
- ✅ Neural controller (learning-based)
- ✅ Blended control mode
- ✅ Stability monitoring (Lyapunov, convergence, oscillation)
- ✅ Performance comparison framework

### O7: Sensory Feedback Encoding (Conceptual) ✓
- ✅ Physical signal encoding strategies
- ✅ Compression and abstraction techniques
- ✅ Documented limitations
